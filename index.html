An app that keeps your eyes healthy.

EyeLeo for Mac is an app that regulary reminds your to take a rest for your eyes, shows you simple eye exercises and prevents you from using Mac during this exercises. When you're working at a computer it's easy to forget about time. This leads to eye strain and other physical problems. EyeLeo guarantees you won't forget to take a break.

Features:

  • Screen blocking during break exercises.
  • Short break with eye exercises.
  • Visual explanation of every exercises.
  • Notifications announcing a long break coming.
  • Strict mode that doesn't allow skipping the breaks.
  • Customizable preferences.
  • and more...
